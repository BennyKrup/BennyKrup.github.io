Beniamin Krupkin

Bioinformatics PhD student interested in transcription regulation, genomics, and genetics. I use deep learning models to study gene regulation in cardiac and neuronal systems in the context of health and disease.

About

I'm a PhD student in the Biological and Medical Informatics (BMI) program at UCSF, in the Ahituv Lab. My research sits at the intersection of deep learning and functional genomics, with a focus on understanding how noncoding genetic variation influences gene regulation in cardiac and neuronal systems.
